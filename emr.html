Embedded Systems & Robotics

Embedded Systems & Robotics Club, NIT Kurukshetra is an earnest endeavor in the automata domain. The brainchild of a diligent student fraternity, the club aims to elevate the standards of the institute by pioneering quality learning and guidance in the field of robotics. A huge student community fancies the world of robotics and the club aids this materialization. The salient feature of the club is that it aims at evolving the seeds of knowledge in this novice chunk, nurturing them into future technocrats. The club welcomes all those with a spark, who crave to dig into this vast promising realm, and ensures a 24x7 lucid mentorship.
